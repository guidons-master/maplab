AGG-Net: Attention Guided Gated-convolutional Network for Depth Image Completion

In this paper, we propose a new model for depth image completion based on the Attention Guided Gated-convolutional Network (AGG-Net), through which more accurate and reliable depth images can be obtained from the raw depth maps and the corresponding RGB images.
